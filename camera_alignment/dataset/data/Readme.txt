Six examples are given, and should unpack into seperate folders.

Each example contains the original images (undistorted).
Sets of left an right points: name_L.txt and name_R.txt
Fundamental matrix F name_F.txt
Two rectification transforms name_H1_H2.txt 
and two rectified images.

Reference paper is 

J. Mallon and P.F. Whelan (2005), 
“Projective rectification from the fundamental matrix”, 
Image and Vision Computing, 
Volume 23, Issue 7 , 1 July 2005, Pages 643-650
